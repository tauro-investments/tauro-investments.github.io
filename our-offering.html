Structuring Services

Due to a profound understanding of the Swiss regulations and commercial law, Tauro Investments can assist in setting up ventures in Switzerland that might serve as holdings or principal companies within a global group set-up. Advantages from a client’s perspective comprise a business friendly environment and an investment climate in a highly reputable economic region. Moreover, Switzerland offers internationally competitive tax rates, which are appreciated by companies from all over the world. We are your strategic partner in capturing the above mentioned benefits. We collaborate with leading Swiss law firms, auditors and custodians to provide you with relevant expertise for optimizing your global business structures.
